Honorable Imaan Sulaiman-Ibrahim

Honorable Minister of Women Affairs

The Honorable Minister leads the Federal Ministry of Women Affairs in its mission to promote gender equality, empower women, and protect the rights of women and children across Nigeria. With extensive experience in public service, she is committed to advancing policies and programs that enhance the welfare and development of women in all spheres of national life.
