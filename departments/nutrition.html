Nutrition

Promoting optimal nutrition for women, children, and families across Nigeria

Overview

The Nutrition Department is dedicated to improving the nutritional status of women and children through evidence-based interventions, education, and policy development.

Key Functions

  • Develop and implement nutrition policies and programs
  • Promote maternal, infant, and young child nutrition
  • Address malnutrition and micronutrient deficiencies
  • Conduct nutrition education and awareness campaigns
  • Collaborate with partners to improve food security
