Gender Affairs

Promoting gender equality and women's empowerment across all sectors

Overview

The Gender Affairs Department is committed to advancing gender equality, women's rights, and the empowerment of women and girls in Nigeria through policy development, advocacy, and strategic partnerships.

Key Functions

  • Formulate and implement gender policies and programs
  • Promote gender mainstreaming in all sectors
  • Advocate for women's rights and gender equality
  • Monitor and evaluate gender-related programs and policies
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to eliminate gender-based discrimination
