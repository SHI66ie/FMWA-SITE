Reform Coordination & Service Improvement

Driving institutional reforms and service delivery improvements for better governance

Overview

The Reform Coordination & Service Improvement Department is responsible for coordinating and implementing public service reforms, ensuring efficient service delivery, and promoting good governance practices within the Ministry.

Key Functions

  • Coordinate the implementation of public service reforms
  • Drive service delivery improvement initiatives
  • Monitor and evaluate reform programs
  • Facilitate capacity building for reform implementation
  • Promote innovation and best practices in service delivery
